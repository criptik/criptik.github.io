About Meta Crosswords:

  • Wall Street Journal's introduction to Meta Solving.
  • Note that the Wall Street Journal has a weekly meta crossword every Friday, these can be accessed (along with other puzzles) thru their Wall Street Journal Crossword Archive.
  • Muller's Monthly Music Meta comes out once a month and the puzzles have a music theme.
  • Ariadne's Crossword Library comes out once a month and the puzzles have a literary theme.
  • Diary of a Crossword Fiend is a good resource for meta solutions (and many other crossword solutions) if you can't figure it out yourself. Solutions are not posted until the associated contest is closed.
